### Create an account

The first step to getting started with Juicebox is creating your account! To do so, click the [Sign In](https://app.juicebox.ai/) button in the top right hand corner of the [Juicebox.ai](https://juicebox.ai/) webpage.

From there, you will have three different options to sign-in:

- Continue with Google
- Continue with Email
- Continue with SSO / SAML\*

\*SSO / SAML is available on the Business plan. Please reach out to your Juicebox representative to learn more.

![Screenshot 2026-02-25 at 4.11.28 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/getting-started/Screenshot_2026-02-25_at_4-11-28_PM.png)

### Verify your email address

When creating an account, you will be prompted to confirm your email address. Head to your inbox and verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to you.

<Tip>
  Can’t find the link to verify your email? Please be sure to check your spam folder. You can also request a new email by selecting “resend email.”
</Tip>

If you are facing any issues with email verification, please [contact support](/contact-support).

### Select your organization name

You will be prompted to choose your organization name. This is used internally, only. Most customers use the name of their company or team, for example Pied Piper or Pied Piper Recruiting.

You can change your organization name later, too, so don’t worry if you’re unsure.

### Integrate your email

To access the entire Juicebox platform, integrate your email from the **Integrations** tab on the left-hand menu. Then sign in with your email provider, and once your email status says **Connected**, you are all good to go!
