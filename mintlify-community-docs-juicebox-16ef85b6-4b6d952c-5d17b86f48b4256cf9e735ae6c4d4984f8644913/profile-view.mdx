You can access profiles in two different views:

- The Sidebar Profile View, accessible by clicking the profile card.
- The Full Profile View, accessible by clicking the name of the candidate, or by selecting “View Full Profile” in the sidebar.

### Sidebar Profile View

View profiles in the right-hand sidebar for a summary of linked socials, contact information, experience, and more.

![Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 5.16.28 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/profile-view/6032b81b-3217-4399-b009-56fd2b95e3e5.png)

### Full Profile View

Selecting the candidate’s name, or clicking on the |← icon within the side profile will expand the candidate’s profile view. This full view will now show additional details such as, profile highlights, notes, and team engagement history.

![Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 5.13.32 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/profile-view/Screenshot_2026-05-11_at_5-13-32_PM.png)

### Profile Tags

See highlights about a profile, including experience, tenure, likelihood to switch, and skills.

Sample profile tags include the following (please note that this is not a comprehensive list of profile tags): Top VC-backed Startup, Fast Career Growth, Web3 Experience, Board Member, Promoted Twice, Early Stage Startup, High Average Tenure, Top 100 Global Uni, etc.

![Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 5.17.41 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/profile-view/Screenshot_2026-05-11_at_5-17-41_PM.png)

### Activity History, AI-Powered File Summaries, & More

Use the **Notes, Sequences, Activity, and Projects** tabs to track past engagement with a candidate and collaborate with your team.

Under the **Notes** tab, you can tag members of your team, write shared comments, or attach documents by clicking **Attach Files** (e.g. PDFs, resumes, screenshots).

![Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 5.20.59 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/profile-view/Screenshot_2026-05-11_at_5-20-59_PM.png)

If you upload a file, you can also generate an AI-powered summary. The summary appears in the notes section and can be saved alongside your comment for future reference by your team.

![Screenshot 2026-05-11 at 5.27.01 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/profile-view/Screenshot_2026-05-11_at_5-27-01_PM.png)

# Keyboard Shortcuts

When the contact side-panel or full profile page is open, the following keyboard shortcuts can be used:

- `left` - Navigate to previous profile
- `right` - Navigate to next profile
- `esc` - Close sidebar (collapses if expanded, closes if collapsed)
- `shift+e` - Export profile
- `shift+m` - Summarize profile
- `shift+o` - Toggle sidebar expansion
- `shift+1` - Switch to Notes tab
- `shift+2` - Switch to Sequences tab
- `shift+3` - Switch to Activity tab
- `shift+4` - Switch to Projects tab
- `shift+5` - Switch to Tasks tab
- `option+shift+s` - Shortlist / Unshortlist
