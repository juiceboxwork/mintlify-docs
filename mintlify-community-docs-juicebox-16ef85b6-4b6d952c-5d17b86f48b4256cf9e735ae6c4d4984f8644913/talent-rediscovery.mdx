Revisiting past applicants, identifying high-intent talent and bringing the right people back into your hiring pipeline has never been easier now that Internal Talent Rediscovery is available in Juicebox!

Since the strongest candidates for your open roles may already be in your ATS, Talent Rediscovery lets you search, filter, and re-engage candidates from your ATS, using data like stages, jobs, and tags to help filter and find the right candidates.

## Getting Started

1. Open a Juicebox project and edit your filters
2. Within Edit Your Search Filters, click on ATS
3. Select Include, Exclude, or Only show ATS candidates based on your use case
   1. If you are only showing ATS candidates, fine-tune your results using Juicebox’s ATS-based filters such as:
      1. Jobs
      2. Tags
      3. Current Stages
      4. Stages
      5. Recruiters
      6. Last Interaction

### **Why Talent Rediscovery Matters**

Recruiting teams often overlook past applicants who were:

- Strong fits but not selected (Silver-medalists)
- Paused due to timing or headcount
- Screened for similar roles in the past
- A little under qualified, but has since gained more experience and skills

### With Talent Rediscovery, you can:

- Save time by starting with warmed up and familiar candidates
- Improve response rates by re-engaging known talent
- Reduce sourcing costs by maximizing your existing ATS data
- Avoid duplicate outreach when sourcing net-new candidates

### Don’t know where to begin? Here’s our quick start on all things Internal Talent Rediscovery:

**When should I use Internal Talent Rediscovery?**

1. Reopening a previously filled role
2. Hiring for a similar position
3. Re-engaging silver-medalist candidates
4. Revisiting applicants who dropped off due to timing

**Which ATS filter should I use?**

1. **Want to revisit past talent?** Use the “Include ATS candidates” or “Only show ATS candidates” filters
2. **Want to only source net-new talent?** Use the “Exclude ATS candidates” to avoid duplicate outreach and keep ATS exports clean
3. **Want to re-engage talent strategically?** Use the additional ATS filters under “Only show ATS candidates” to review candidates from certain stages, jobs, or tags within your ATS
