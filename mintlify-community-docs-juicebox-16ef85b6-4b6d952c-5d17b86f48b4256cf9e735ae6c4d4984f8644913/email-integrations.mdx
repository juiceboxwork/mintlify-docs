## Email Integration Overview

Juicebox supports integrations with Gmail, Microsoft and IMAP providers. To connect a new email address or update an existing integration, please navigate to the **Integrations** tab on the left-hand menu. From there, you can connect or disconnect an email account by securely signing in via your email provider.

![Integrations page for KB ](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/email-integrations/Integrations_page_for_KB_.png)

<Note>
  Juicebox **does not** use customer data for our candidate/profile database in any way. Email integrations are used **only** for the following purposes:

  1. Sending email sequences to candidates
  2. Stopping sequences when a candidate replies to a thread
  3. Auto-drafting replies to candidate responses

  For more details, please see our [privacy policy](https://juicebox.ai/privacy-policy#other-sites-and-services)
</Note>

## Internal & SOBO (Send on Behalf of) Setup

Juicebox also supports integrating **internal** (your own) and **SOBO** (external) mailboxes. Each mailbox will be able to send anywhere from 20-500 emails per day (plan dependent), and is a great method to utilize to maximize candidate outreach and deliverability.

![Add a mailbox for KB ](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/email-integrations/Add_a_mailbox_for_KB_.png)

**To add an additional mailbox:**

1. Select **Integrations** from the left-hand menu
2. Select **Add a mailbox**
   1. To connect your own email address, select **Connect a Gmail account,** **Connect an Outlook account** or **Connect via IMAP** depending on your email provider
   2. To connect an external/SOBO mailbox, select **Add a SOBO mailbox** (The external user will then receive an invite from Juicebox that they have to accept in order for their mailbox to be available for use)
3. Once accepted, status will change from **Pending** to **Connected**

_If the email status is stuck pending after an invite is accepted, please ensure that user’s email is not currently in use by another Juicebox account._

### **Internal Mailboxes**

Internal mailboxes are email addresses you personally own or actively manage and use in your day-to-day work. These mailboxes are ideal for recruiter-led outreach and hands-on candidate engagement.

Common internal mailbox use cases include:

- Your primary work email
- Client-specific or branded domains
- Mailbox aliases

### **SOBO Mailboxes**

SOBO mailboxes allow you to send on behalf of other stakeholders, such as hiring managers, executives, or teammates, without them having to be hands on in the outreach process. This makes it easy to personalize outreach at scale while maintaining full visibility into candidate conversations and reply history.

With SOBO mailboxes, emails will appear to come directly from that external address, and if a candidate replies to that message, **both the external mailbox owner and the Juicebox user who initiated the message will be notified**, ensuring no response is missed.

Common SOBO use cases include:

- Sending on behalf of a hiring manager to boost reply rates
- Executive-led or founder-branded outreach
- Coordinated recruiting efforts where recruiters manage execution, but stakeholders stay informed

For more information on how many mailboxes can be connected to your account, please refer to your plan below:

|  | **Free** | **Starter** | **Growth** | **Business** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **# of Mailboxes** | - | 1 mailbox | 3 per seat | 6 per seat |

## Mailbox Settings

To edit the settings of a specific mailbox, please click on the three horizontal dots on the far right side of the integrated mailbox.  \*\*\*\*

![Mailbox settings for KB ](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/email-integrations/Mailbox_settings_for_KB_.png)

From the mailbox settings page, you can:

- Change the sender name to reflect who the email address or mailbox belongs to
- Increase or decrease the max emails sent per day (daily send limit must be between 20 and 500 depending on your plan)
- Manage auto-drafted responses
- Change the email’s access level
- Manage sequence behavior when a meeting is booked

For more information on auto-drafts, access levels and sequence behavior, please continue reading below.

![Edit mailbox settings for KB](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/email-integrations/Edit_mailbox_settings_for_KB.png)

### Auto-drafts

Juicebox has the ability to create auto-drafted responses in your mailbox or Juicebox directly. The quality of these drafts will improve over time, as it learns from your edits, interactions and sent responses.

To turn auto-draft on, please click the checkbox next to **Create drafts in my mailbox.** This can be turned on or off at any point in time, and will only create a drafted message in response to your candidate.

**To view your drafted response in Juicebox, you can:**

1. Open the replied candidate’s profile \> select **Sequences** \> select **Draft available**
   ![image](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/email-integrations/image.png)
2. From the Juicebox email notification, select **Your AI draft response is ready — click to review and respond.**

**To view your drafted response within your mailbox, you can:**

1. Select **reply** and the draft will appear!

### Public & Private Mailboxes

Control who can send a sequence from a mailbox with public and private access levels. Anyone will be able to create or edit a sequence using a mailbox, but only users with access to that mailbox can initiate a sequence run.

![Screenshot 2026-01-23 at 3.44.23 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/email-integrations/Screenshot_2026-01-23_at_3-44-23_PM.png)

- **Public (default):** Anyone in the organization can send sequences using this mailbox
- **Private (and shared):** Only the mailbox owner and explicitly shared users can send sequences using this mailbox

### Sequence Behavior

To automatically stop sequences and archive any pending tasks from this mailbox when a candidate books a meeting, please select the checkbox next to **Stop sequences when meeting is booked.**

## Admin Email Settings

Manage email settings for your entire organization from **Settings \> Organization** with your Juicebox account.

### Organization Email Sending Limit

While individual users can increase their daily email limit up to 200, org admins can override this limit from **Settings \> Organization \> Organization Email Sending Limit** and set a maximum daily limit of up to 500 emails per mailbox (depending on your plan).

Once that limit is set by an admin, individual users can then go back into their Integrations tab, and change their max daily emails up to that new limit.

![Screenshot 2026-06-24 at 1.56.41 PM](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juiceboxwork/docs/kb-migration/images/email-integrations/Screenshot_2026-06-24_at_1-56-41_PM.png)

### Default Mailbox Access

As a Juicebox admin, you can also set a default access level for all _newly_ connected mailboxes through **Settings → Organization → Default Mailbox Access**. Please note, existing mailbox integrations will not be affected and mailbox owners can change privacy settings through the Integrations tab.
